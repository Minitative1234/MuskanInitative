There are about 189.2 million undernourished people in India; majority of women and children. Malnutrition and widespread prevalence of stunting, wasting and nutritional deficiencies among women and children are recognised elements of India’s profile in the Global Hunger Index. In 2021 India has 138 crores population in India. India’s Women Malnutrition Profile According to Government figures from the year 2015 - 2016, 22.9 percent of women in the 15-29 age group are underweight as compared to 20.2 percent of men in the same age group. According to the state of food security and nutrition in the world 2020 report in 2016, nearly 51.4 percent of women of reproductive age in India were suffering from Anemia.
